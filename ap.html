AndhraPradesh

Andhra Pradesh , also known as Andhra or Andhra Rashtram, [12][13] is a state in the south-eastern coastal region of India. [14] It is the seventh-largest state by area covering an area of 162,975 km2 (62,925 sq mi)[6] and tenth-most populous state with 49,386,799 inhabitants[15][16]. It is bordered by Odisha to the north, Chhattisgarh to the north-east, Tamil Nadu to the south, Karnataka to the west, Telangana to the north-west and the Bay of Bengal to the east. [17] It has the second longest coastline in India after Gujarat of about 974 km[18]. Andhra Pradesh is the first state to be formed on a linguistic basis in India on 1 October 1953.
